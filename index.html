About

Dr Zhaoxin Ren is a Senior Lecturer in Aerospace Engineering at Swansea University. His research focuses on cryogenic hydrogen thermofluids, multiphase CFD, digital twins, hydrogen safety, combustion, advanced propulsion, and sustainable aviation.

Research Interests

  • Cryogenic hydrogen thermofluids
  • Multiphase CFD and digital twins
  • Hydrogen safety and combustion
  • Advanced propulsion and detonation physics
  • Thermal management and cryogenic heat exchangers
  • Sustainable aviation and clean energy systems

  3. Ren Z. and Zheng L. Numerical study on rotating detonation stability in two-phase kerosene-air mixture. Combustion and Flame, 2021.
  4. Ren Z. et al. Effect of the multiphase composition in a premixed fuel-air stream on wedge-induced oblique detonation stabilisation. Journal of Fluid Mechanics, 2018.
